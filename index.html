About Me

Adapting to changes and learning new things is something I am very familiar with. Immigrating from the Dominican Republic to the US at a young age I had to adapt to a new environment and learn a completely new language. As I became proficient in english, my parents soon became dependent on my support with translating. My parents taught me to keep pushing until you persevere, so in 2018 I was the first in my family to obtain a college degree in Digital Studio Arts. Studying the arts has allowed me to be adaptable and implement a creative approach to different projects. I want to continue being the first and bring the drive I have for success not only to my community, but to your company.
